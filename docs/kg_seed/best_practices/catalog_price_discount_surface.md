title: "Surface the discount or price point in the headline when running a promotional campaign"

brand_id: null
metadata:
  category: catalog
  evidence_source: "Drive corpus — Nature's Touch '25% Off All Cosmetic Creams', Botanical Republic 'Get Discount Up To 25%' 2026-05"
  applicable_when: "promotional banners with price reduction offer"
---

When a discount is available, it is the primary reason to click — place the percentage in the headline. "Get 25% Off All Cosmetic Creams" (Nature's Touch) and "Get Discount Up To 25%" (Botanical Republic) work because the number and product category appear together, answering two key shopper questions: how much, and on what. Body copy handles the reassurance narrative: "Pamper your skin with our nourishing cosmetic creams — crafted for radiant, silky-smooth results." Discount banners without a visible percentage in the headline perform measurably below category benchmarks for beauty ecommerce.
