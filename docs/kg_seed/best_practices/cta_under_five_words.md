Every CTA in the beauty banner corpus is four words or fewer: "Shop Now", "Explore Collection", "Buy Now", "Browse Product", "Explore Now →", "Book Now". Short CTAs scan in a single fixation on mobile, while CTAs like "Click here to shop all products" add cognitive overhead. For service bookings, "Book Now" outperforms "Schedule Your Appointment" because it matches the user's mental shorthand for the action. The arrow suffix ("Explore Now →") works well on discovery CTAs to signal forward navigation without adding word count.
