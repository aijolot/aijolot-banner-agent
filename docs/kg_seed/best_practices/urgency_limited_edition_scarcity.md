Kylie Jenner's lip product launch uses "limited-edition" in supporting copy and "NOW AVAILABLE" as the launch badge — creating urgency through exclusivity rather than price. Scarcity language ("limited-edition", "exclusive", "only while stocks last") maintains brand price integrity while motivating purchase action. Combine scarcity copy with a product-specific CTA ("shop now" pointing to the product PDP) rather than a generic collection CTA, to reduce friction between the urgency signal and the purchase action. For Shopify implementation, connect the banner to a product metafield that auto-removes the "limited-edition" badge when inventory falls to zero.
