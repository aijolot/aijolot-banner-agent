The skincare brand surfaces two proof points directly in the hero: "400+ New Collection" and "10k+ Satisfied Customer." The cruelty-free brand testimonial — "'I'm so happy to find a cruelty-free brand that actually works. My skin feels soft and hydrated!' — Nusrat Jahan, Product designer" — adds credibility via a named reviewer with a job title. Social proof in the hero is most effective for cold audiences with no prior brand exposure. For warm audiences (retargeting), replace proof metrics with product-specific copy — they already trust the brand and need a reason to return, not validation.
