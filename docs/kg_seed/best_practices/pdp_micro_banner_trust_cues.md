At the moment a shopper hovers over Add to Cart, an unspoken hesitation kicks in — is this store legitimate, is my payment safe, will the item actually arrive? Small "micro-banners" — compact blocks of confidence cues with icons for secure checkout, accepted payment methods, free/fast shipping, and returns or guarantee — answer those questions in a single glance and measurably reduce that hesitation. The decisive factor is placement: the cues work when they sit at the point of purchase (below the price, beside or just under the buy button), not buried in the footer. Keep each cue to an icon plus 2–4 words, group them as one tidy strip, and don't let them visually outshout the CTA itself. This is a low-cost, high-leverage PDP conversion lever distinct from a promotional offer strip.
