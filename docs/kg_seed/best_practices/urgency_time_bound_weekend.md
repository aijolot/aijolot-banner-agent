The flash sale banner demonstrates the correct urgency pattern: a bold "FLASH SALE" badge paired with the time constraint "THIS WEEKEND ONLY" communicates both the magnitude and the deadline of the offer in two lines. Generic banners without a time bound ("Big Savings") underperform against time-anchored copy. The time constraint must appear in the headline or immediately below it — not in the footer or disclaimer — so it is visible above the fold without scrolling. After the offer window closes, swap the urgency headline for the standard brand message to avoid confusing returning visitors with an expired promotion.
