title: "Generous whitespace signals premium brand positioning and improves copy legibility"

brand_id: null

---

Drunk Elephant and WESSAINT AURENT both use wide padding, limited element counts, and controlled negative space to signal premium quality. Dense banner layouts with multiple badge overlays, competing headlines, and tightly packed copy communicate discount-brand positioning regardless of actual price point. For brands targeting mid-market and above, allow at least 20% of the banner area to remain empty, restrict element count to headline + supporting line + CTA + product image, and use generous line-height (1.6+) on supporting copy to reinforce the premium feel.
