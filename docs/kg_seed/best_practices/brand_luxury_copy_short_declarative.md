WESSAINT AURENT: "we believe that beauty is more than just skin deep. It's a feeling of confidence, a reflection of self-care, and an expression." — short declarative sentences with no discount language, price points, or promotional frames. Drunk Elephant frames its identity as "performance and personality can live in the same jar" — a statement of philosophy, not a product claim. Luxury banners earn trust through conviction of voice: introduce no urgency language, no percentages, and no sale badges in the hero area. The CTA on luxury banners is always a discovery verb — "DISCOVER MORE" or "LEARN MORE" — not a transaction verb like "Buy Now."
