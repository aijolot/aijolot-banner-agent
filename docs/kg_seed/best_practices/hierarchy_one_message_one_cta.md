---
title: "Hero banners convert best with one message, one proof point, and one CTA"
kind: best_practice
brand_id: null
metadata:
  category: hierarchy
  evidence_source: "Drive corpus — Bearen, NaturaGlow, Kylie Jenner banners 2026-05"
  applicable_when: "all hero and collection banners"
---

Every high-performing banner in the beauty corpus leads with a single benefit statement ("Glow Beyond Beauty", "Nourishing Soap"), supports it with one descriptive line, and closes with exactly one CTA button. Banners that introduce competing promotions — sale badge + new arrival + CTA — lose click-through focus across beauty verticals. Reserve the hero area for a single offer or brand message and move secondary promotions to promo cards or announcement bars.
