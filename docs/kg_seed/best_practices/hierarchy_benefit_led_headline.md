title: "Lead the headline with the consumer benefit, not the product category"

Benefit-led headlines like "Glow Beyond Beauty" and "The Drunk Difference" outperform category-first headlines like "Skincare Products" by anchoring the consumer on the outcome, not the SKU. The supporting line should name the product or mechanism: "skin-smart formulas developed with efficacious levels of active ingredients." For promotional banners, the benefit is the discount outcome — "radiant, silky-smooth results" — not the offer mechanic. Drunk Elephant and Bearen both place product names only in secondary positions after the benefit hook is established.
