Bearen uses "Clean, cruelty-free, and glow-boosting" in a single line — three specific claims joined by commas — while NaturaGlow states "100% natural, sustainably sourced ingredients." Both patterns work because the claims are concrete rather than vague ("natural" alone is ambiguous; "100% natural, sustainably sourced" is verifiable). The eco-friendly skincare brand leads with "thoughtfully crafted to nourish, protect, and enhance your skin" — three benefit verbs instead of three claims. Claim statements belong in the supporting line, not the headline, and must not exceed one line at mobile font sizes to avoid text reflow.
