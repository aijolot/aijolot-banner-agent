title: "The CTA button must be visible above the fold on a 390×844 mobile screen without scrolling"

On a 390×844 iPhone 14 viewport with a standard Shopify header (~56px), the visible hero area is approximately 390×788px. The CTA must appear within this area. Stacked layouts that push the product image full-height above the fold and place the headline and CTA below will lose the majority of mobile impressions from users who do not scroll. Limit product image height to 60% of mobile viewport height (~470px) and place the headline + CTA within the remaining 40% (~318px). The Bearen and NaturaGlow banners both keep copy and CTA above the 788px threshold.
