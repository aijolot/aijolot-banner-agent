Bearen uses "Explore Collection", the eco skincare brand uses "Shop Our Collection", and the skincare essentials brand uses "Explore Now →" — all discovery-framed CTAs that invite browsing rather than demanding a transaction. "Buy Now" is appropriate when the banner links to a single product PDP; on collection pages it creates a mismatch between the CTA promise and the destination (a grid, not a buy button), increasing bounce. Use "Explore" or "Shop" for collection destinations and "Shop [Product Name]" for product-level hero destinations.
