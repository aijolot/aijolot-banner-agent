---
title: "Supporting copy in a hero banner should be two lines maximum at desktop sizes"
kind: best_practice
brand_id: null
metadata:
  category: hierarchy
  evidence_source: "Drive corpus — beauty banner audit 2026-05"
  applicable_when: "hero_main, collection_header placements"
---

All strong-performing banners in the beauty corpus keep supporting copy to 2 lines at 16–18px: "Clean, cruelty-free, and glow-boosting products for every shade of beauty." Longer descriptions belong on PDP or About sections — placing them in the hero competes with the CTA and forces text reflow on mobile. If the brand story requires more words, use an About Us section banner with a "Discover More" CTA (as WESSAINT AURENT does) rather than expanding hero copy beyond two lines.
