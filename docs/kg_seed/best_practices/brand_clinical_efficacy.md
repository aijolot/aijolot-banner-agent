Drunk Elephant's hero copy names the mechanism: "skin-smart formulas developed with efficacious levels of active ingredients that can be easily absorbed and put to use." The Ordinary uses "CLINICAL WITH INTEGRITY" as a positioning headline. These brands do not use lifestyle imagery or aspirational language — they use specificity and transparency. For clinical brands, the banner body copy should reference an ingredient, clinical outcome, or formulation principle rather than a generic beauty benefit. Avoid phrases like "best skin ever" or "radiant glow" in clinical brand contexts — they read as category noise rather than brand signal.
