The bath salts banner uses the product in context — "BERGAMOT & YLANG YLANG BATH SALTS 300g with plastic scoop" — surrounded by natural ingredients rather than as a plain pack shot on white. NaturaGlow Body Wash shows the bottle in a styled bathroom setting. Bearen pairs its product with a lifestyle glow visual. In-context imagery increases purchase intent by helping the shopper visualise product use and reduces the visual gap between ad and ownership. For beauty products, in-context means the product beside a textured surface, held by a hand, or alongside complementary ingredients — not floating on a white background.
