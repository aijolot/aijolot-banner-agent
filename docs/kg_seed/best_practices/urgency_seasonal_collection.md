Kylie Jenner's fragrance banner — "THE SCENT OF THE SUMMER / a sweet, warm floral scent... perfect for summer. shop now" — creates urgency by anchoring the product to a finite cultural season without any discount. Seasonal language signals that the product's relevance window is limited, which functions as soft urgency and protects brand price integrity. Seasonal hooks are most effective for beauty and lifestyle categories where the product is genuinely tied to weather, occasion, or calendar moment. Reserve this pattern for collection launches; avoid applying seasonal language to evergreen SKUs to prevent shopper confusion in off-season months.
