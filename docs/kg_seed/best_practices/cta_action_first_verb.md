title: "Start CTAs with an action verb — Shop, Explore, Discover, Buy, or Book"

metadata:
  category: cta
  evidence_source: "Drive corpus — Kylie Jenner 'shop now', Drunk Elephant 'SHOP ALL SKIN CARE', NaturaGlow 'BUY NOW', Bearen 'Explore Collection' 2026-05"
  applicable_when: "all banner CTAs"
---

Every high-intent CTA in the corpus opens with a verb: "Shop", "Explore", "Discover", "Buy", "Book", "Browse". Noun-led CTAs such as "New Collection" or "25% Off" communicate information rather than directing an action, resulting in lower click-through. Verb capitalisation style should match brand tone — Kylie Jenner uses all-lowercase "shop now", Drunk Elephant uses all-caps "SHOP ALL SKIN CARE", and natural beauty brands like Bearen prefer title case "Explore Collection". Maintain consistent capitalisation style across all banners for the same brand.
