title: "Place the percentage discount in the headline rather than in the description"

---

Both Nature's Touch ("Get 25% Off All Cosmetic Creams") and Botanical Republic ("Get Discount Up To 25%") surface the discount percentage as the headline. Burying the discount in the body copy — "Enjoy 25% off today" — reduces offer salience for users who scan rather than read. When the offer includes a range ("Up To 25%"), place the range in the headline. When the discount is fixed, lead with the specific number: "25% Off" performs better than "Up to a quarter off" because numbers are processed faster than written approximations.
