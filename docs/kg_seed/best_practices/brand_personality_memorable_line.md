Drunk Elephant's "performance and personality can live in the same jar" communicates brand values in one sentence — memorable, specific to the brand, and not attributable to any competitor. Generic headlines like "Your Best Skin Starts Here" or "Beauty You Can Trust" are interchangeable across brands and build zero recall. Invest in one original brand voice line per campaign and use it consistently across hero, collection, and PDP banners to build recognition through repetition. When briefing the banner agent, include the brand's core philosophical statement as a tone anchor rather than a generic category description.
