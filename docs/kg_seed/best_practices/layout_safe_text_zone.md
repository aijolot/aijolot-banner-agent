Text placed directly over a product's focal point — the face, the product label, the ingredient hero shot — becomes unreadable when the image is cropped for different viewports. Safe zones are typically the left third or right third of the image frame, away from subject and image edges. The BRANCY hero achieves this with a decorative text-shape element at the edge and the product image isolated in the right column, keeping copy consistently clear at all breakpoints. Define safe zone boundaries in the Shopify section schema as percentage-based padding rather than fixed pixel values.
