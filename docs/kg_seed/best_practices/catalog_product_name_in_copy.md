---

NaturaGlow names the product twice: "Nourishing Soap" as the headline and "NaturaGlow Body Wash" as the product name in the copy. Kylie Jenner names the product in supporting copy: "coconut water lip stain delivers lasting comfort and up to 24-hour moisture." The bath salts banner includes the full product composition — "BERGAMOT & YLANG YLANG BATH SALTS 300g." Specific product names improve click quality: the shopper arrives at the PDP already knowing what they are buying, which reduces bounce rate. Avoid "our bestselling cream" or "new collection" — these phrases are interchangeable across every beauty brand.
