Flash Sale template: badge "FLASH SALE" + subhead "THIS WEEKEND ONLY" + lorem body copy + CTA "Shop Now." Nature's Touch: headline "Get 25% Off All Cosmetic Creams" + body "Pamper your skin with our nourishing cosmetic creams — crafted for radiant, silky-smooth results. Enjoy 25% off today." + CTA "Browse Product." Botanical Republic: "Get Discount Up To 25%" + multi-SKU product carousel. Common tone markers: urgency-first hierarchy, numeric discount prominent in headline, body copy provides product or offer context, CTA always transactional. Typography in this archetype uses all-caps or bold for the urgency badge, normal weight for body. Use only for genuine time-limited or quantity-limited offers — overuse dilutes the urgency signal and conditions the audience to wait for sales.
