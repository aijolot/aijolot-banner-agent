WESSAINT AURENT About banner: headline "ABOUT US", body "we believe that beauty is more than just skin deep. It's a feeling of confidence, a reflection of self-care, and an expression.", CTA "DISCOVER MORE". Tone markers: lowercase brand copy despite all-caps brand name (deliberate contrast), philosophical framing ("more than just skin deep"), no product names, no prices, no discounts, open-ended CTA. The use of "an expression" as the final clause — a sentence fragment — is intentional; it leaves the thought open for the consumer to complete. This voice archetype is appropriate for brand introduction pages, About banners, and high-funnel awareness campaigns where the goal is emotional connection rather than immediate transaction.
