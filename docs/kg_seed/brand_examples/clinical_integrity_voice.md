Drunk Elephant hero: "The Drunk Difference — Drunk Elephant believes performance and personality can live in the same jar. Skin-smart formulas developed with efficacious levels of active ingredients that can be easily absorbed and put to use." CTAs: "SHOP ALL SKIN CARE" (primary, all-caps filled button) + "LEARN MORE" (secondary text). The Ordinary: "CLINICAL WITH INTEGRITY." Key tone markers: specificity ("efficacious levels", "easily absorbed"), intellectual confidence, no lifestyle language, transparency-led positioning. Avoid discount language entirely in this voice. Secondary banner variation: "Discover In-Store Consultations — One-on-one consultations have arrived. Expert guidance tailored to your skin's needs. Book Now / Learn More" — same pattern applied to service, dual CTA with same hierarchy. Use for educated beauty consumers who research formulations.
