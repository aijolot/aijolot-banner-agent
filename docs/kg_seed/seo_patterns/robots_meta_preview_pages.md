The banner agent generates preview URLs for human review (HITL node 10) before publishing to the live Shopify theme. These preview pages must not be indexed by Google, or they may appear in search results as duplicate or unfinished content, or worse, reveal promotional copy before the campaign launch. Pattern: when the banner is in draft or preview state, render `<meta name="robots" content="noindex, nofollow">` in the page head. This tag must be removed automatically when the campaign publishes to production. The banner agent's Shopify theme publish node should verify the robots meta tag is absent in the published output. A preview page that is accidentally left with noindex after publish will not rank — this is a quieter failure than inadvertent pre-launch indexing but still reduces campaign SEO impact.
